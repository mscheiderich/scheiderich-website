Community Involvement

The Scheiderich Agency is proud to call Buford home. Beyond insurance, we believe in investing in the people and places that make this community what it is — through local partnerships, sponsorships, and hands-on support.

Local Partnership

Our Partnership with the Brightside Forever Foundation

Right here in Buford, the Brightside Forever Foundation is working to close a gap most people never notice.

Source: Brightside Forever Foundation.

The foundation's program, The Bean — the Brightside Empower Ability Network — partners with local businesses to change that. The Bean trains and mentors employers, then connects them with motivated candidates who are ready to work. The Scheiderich Agency is a proud member.

Our Team Member

Meet Natalie

When we joined The Bean, we were introduced to Natalie. After interviewing her, we hired her to handle the social media for our office — and she's been one of the best things to happen to us.

She's brought an energy to that work that's hard to teach. She's also, simply put, the most motivated team member we've ever had. She loves coming to work, and it shows — she lights up the entire office, and our whole team looks forward to seeing her every day.

We share this for one reason: to show other business owners that hiring through The Bean isn't charity — it's a great hire. Natalie makes our business better, and there are more people like her on Brightside's waiting list right now.

Bring The Bean to Your Business

If you run a business in the Buford area, we'd encourage you to take a look. The Bean gives member businesses guidance on hiring, ongoing mentorship, and access to a list of ready candidates. It's a straightforward way to make our community more inclusive — and to find dependable people who genuinely want to be there.
