Lake Lanier Homeowners: What to Know

Properties adjacent to or near Lake Lanier can face higher insurance premiums, stricter underwriting requirements, and coverage gaps that standard policies do not address. Flood zone designations, dock and boathouse structures, and watercraft liability are all factors worth reviewing with your agent.

If you own a home near the lake or are purchasing one, call us before closing. A 20-minute conversation can surface coverage questions worth addressing before your policy is in place.
