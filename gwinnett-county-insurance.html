A Local Agency That Understands Gwinnett County

Gwinnett County is the most populous county in Georgia outside of Fulton, with over one million residents spanning dozens of distinct communities. From the established neighborhoods near Lawrenceville to the fast-growing areas around Suwanee and Sugar Hill, Gwinnett is home to an incredibly diverse mix of homeowners, renters, families, and business owners — all with different insurance needs.

The county has grown rapidly over the past two decades, and with that growth comes real complexity around property values, traffic density, and the variety of housing types. Ranch homes, newer construction, townhomes, and investment properties all carry different coverage considerations. Our team helps Gwinnett County residents understand what their policies actually cover — and where the gaps are before something goes wrong.

We serve clients across Gwinnett County from our Downtown Buford office. You do not need to come to us — we handle quotes and coverage reviews by phone and email. But when you call, you reach a licensed agent, not a call center.

Common Questions

Insurance Questions from Gwinnett County Residents

Do you serve clients throughout Gwinnett County or only in Buford?
We serve clients throughout all of Gwinnett County including Lawrenceville, Duluth, Suwanee, Snellville, Norcross, Sugar Hill, Dacula, Grayson, and surrounding communities. Our office is in Downtown Buford and most client interactions happen by phone or email.
Why does auto insurance cost more in Gwinnett County than in rural Georgia?
Population density and traffic volume directly affect insurance rates. Gwinnett County has significant traffic on I-85, SR-316, and local corridors like Sugarloaf Parkway. Higher traffic density means higher accident frequency, which is reflected in regional pricing. We help clients understand their options and identify the coverage that makes sense for their situation.
My home has increased significantly in value — should I update my homeowners policy?
Yes. Gwinnett County has seen strong property value growth, and many homeowners are underinsured simply because their coverage was set years ago and has not kept pace with current rebuild costs. We offer no-charge insurance reviews to help identify gaps like this.
Does your agency serve renters in Gwinnett County?
Yes. Renters insurance is an affordable and often overlooked coverage. Many Gwinnett County renters assume their landlord's policy covers their belongings — it does not. We help renters understand what coverage they need and how to get it.
Can I get a quote without visiting your Buford office?
Absolutely. The majority of our Gwinnett County clients never visit our office. We handle everything by phone, email, or online form. Call 770-268-4418 or use the contact form on this page.
Do you offer business insurance for Gwinnett County small businesses?
Yes. We work with small business owners throughout Gwinnett County on general liability, business owner policies (BOP), and commercial coverage. Many small businesses are underinsured or have coverage gaps they are not aware of until they file a claim.
