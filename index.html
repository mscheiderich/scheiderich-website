Frequently Asked Questions

What types of insurance does The Scheiderich Agency offer?
Our team helps clients with home, auto, life, and business insurance. We also offer bundled coverage options and can review your current policies to identify any gaps or changes worth considering.
Is The Scheiderich Agency only for Buford residents?
No — we serve clients across Georgia. Our office is in Downtown Buford, and we work with homeowners, families, drivers, and business owners throughout Gwinnett County, Metro Atlanta, and the broader state.
How do I get started with a quote?
The easiest way is to call us at 770-268-4418 or submit a quote request online. We typically respond the same day. There is no pressure or obligation — we start with a conversation about what you need.
Can I get a review of my current insurance?
Yes. An insurance review is one of the most useful conversations our team has with clients — especially after a home purchase, new vehicle, marriage, or any major life change. Contact us to schedule one at no cost.
