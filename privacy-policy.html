This Privacy Policy applies to thescheiderichagency.com and all pages and tools hosted on that domain.

Overview

The Scheiderich Agency ("we," "our," or "us") is a licensed insurance agency located at 60 W Park St, Buford, Georgia 30518. We are committed to protecting the privacy of visitors to our website. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

By using this website, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the site.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you submit a contact form, request a quote, or otherwise reach out to us through this website, we may collect:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • The reason for your inquiry
  • Any additional information you voluntarily include in your message

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as Social Security numbers, financial account numbers, or health information through this website.

Information From Our Calculator Tools

Our life insurance calculator is a client-side tool. The information you enter — such as income, debt amounts, and household details — is processed entirely in your browser to generate an estimate. This information is not transmitted to or stored on our servers unless you subsequently submit a contact form.

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, certain information is collected automatically through analytics tools and standard web server technology, including:

  • Your IP address and general geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search terms that led you to our site
  • Date and time of your visit

How We Use Your Information

Information you provide through our contact forms is used to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or quote request
  • Contact you to schedule an appointment or insurance review
  • Provide you with information about insurance products relevant to your inquiry
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations applicable to licensed insurance agents in Georgia

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. We do not use your information to send unsolicited commercial communications unrelated to your inquiry.

Analytics & Tracking

We use third-party analytics services — including but not limited to Google Analytics and other website analytics platforms — to understand how visitors use our website. These services may collect information about your browsing behavior on our site and across other websites.

Analytics data is used in aggregate to improve our website content, understand which pages are most useful to visitors, and measure the effectiveness of our online presence. We do not use analytics data to identify individual visitors for marketing purposes.

Third-party analytics providers operate under their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any analytics providers whose practices concern you. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

You may opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Third-Party Services

This website uses third-party services to provide certain functionality. These providers may process information about your use of our site in accordance with their own privacy policies. Categories of third-party services we use include:

  • Form processing services — to receive and route contact form submissions to our team
  • Website analytics platforms — to understand site traffic and visitor behavior
  • Review display services — to display verified client reviews on our website
  • Font and web resource providers — to deliver design assets used on the site

We select third-party providers that maintain reasonable data security practices. However, we are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services and encourage you to review their individual policies.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. Our website may use cookies in the following ways:

  • Essential cookies — required for basic site functionality
  • Analytics cookies — placed by our analytics providers to track site usage patterns
  • Preference cookies — to remember settings or preferences during your visit

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can configure your browser to refuse all cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of some website features.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information submitted through this website against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our website uses HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted between your browser and our servers.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. If you have reason to believe that your interaction with us is no longer secure, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

Information submitted through our contact forms is retained for as long as necessary to fulfill your inquiry and for a reasonable period thereafter for business and regulatory recordkeeping purposes. As a licensed insurance agency in Georgia, we are subject to state record retention requirements that may govern how long certain communications are maintained.

Analytics data collected through third-party providers is retained according to the data retention settings of those platforms.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Opt out of certain data collection or processing activities

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in the Contact section below. We will respond to verifiable requests within a reasonable timeframe consistent with applicable law.

Residents of California and other states with specific consumer privacy laws may have additional rights under those laws. We will honor requests consistent with applicable state law.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed to children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us and we will promptly delete that information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or for other operational reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of our website following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
